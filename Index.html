Fun Facts About The New York Yankees

    - The New York Yankees last won the World Series in 2009
    - The Yankees have had a winning season for 33 consecutive years, dating back to 1993
    - The Yankees have had 24 players inducted as Yankees to the Baseball Hall of Fame

Anyways, the Yankees have some of my favorite baseball players playing for them and they are based in New York which is where I am from. These are some of the reasons why the New York Yankees are my favorite baseball team.
